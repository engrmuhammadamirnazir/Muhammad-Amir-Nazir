# PRJ — Odoo-Shopify Connector

> Development of a superior Odoo-Shopify connector module (`odoo_shopify_automation`) for versions 17 and 18.

---

## Overview

Ecosire is developing a comprehensive Odoo-Shopify connector designed to outperform existing solutions from Emipro, Setu, Webkul, and Teqstars on the Odoo App Store. The module enables seamless synchronization between Shopify eCommerce and Odoo ERP.

## Key Features

### Integration & Synchronization
- Real-time synchronization of orders, products, customers, stock, and prices
- Webhook support for instant data updates
- Bidirectional sync between Shopify and Odoo

### Planned Capabilities
- Product sync (variants, images, pricing, inventory)
- Order management (creation, fulfillment, returns)
- Customer data sync
- Inventory level management
- Multi-store support
- Payment gateway integration
