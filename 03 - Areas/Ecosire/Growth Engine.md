# Growth Engine

> 11-app sales & marketing automation suite within the [[ECOSIRE.COM Platform]].

---

## Overview

The Growth Engine is Ecosire's bundled SaaS product suite for sales automation, marketing, and customer engagement. Part of the [[ECOSIRE.COM Platform]] monorepo.

---

## Applications (11 Apps)

### Phase 1 — Foundation
- **Workflows** — Marketing automation engine
- **Lead Scoring** — AI-powered lead qualification
- **Contact Events** — Activity tracking and history
- **SMS** — Bulk and transactional messaging
- **WhatsApp** — Business messaging integration
- **Unified Inbox** — Conversations across channels

### Phase 2 — Capture & Convert
- **Funnels** — Sales funnel builder
- **Pages** — Landing page builder
- **Checkout** — Payment processing

### Phase 3 — Engage & Retain
- **Calendar** — Appointment scheduling
- **Chat** — Live chat widget
- **Affiliates** — Referral program management
- **Loyalty** — Customer rewards

### Phase 4 — Intelligence
- **Analytics** — Reporting and insights dashboard

---

## Technical Details

Built as 14 NestJS backend modules within the [[ECOSIRE.COM Platform]]:
`workflows`, `scoring`, `contact-events`, `sms`, `whatsapp`, `conversations`, `funnels`, `pages`, `checkout`, `calendar`, `chat`, `affiliates`, `loyalty`, `analytics`
