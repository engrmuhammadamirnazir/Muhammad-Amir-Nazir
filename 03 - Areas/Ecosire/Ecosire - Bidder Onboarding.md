# Ecosire — Bidder Onboarding

> Onboarding guide for new bidders at [[Ecosire Private Limited]].

---

## Role Overview

As a bidder, responsibilities include:
- Identifying the right job opportunities
- Submitting high-quality, customized bids
- Maintaining accurate records of work

Success is measured by quality, consistency, conversion, and volume.

---

## Tools Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Gather Job Extension** | Copying data from job boards |
| **Google Sheet** | Tracking bids |
| **Job Platforms** | Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Dice, LinkedIn, etc. |
| **Virtual Machine** | All-in-one work environment |

---

## Process

1. Extension auto-grabs job link and title
2. Bidder reads full job description
3. Submit customized, high-quality bid
4. Track in Google Sheet
5. Follow up and maintain records
