# SEO & Digital Marketing

> Area note for all digital marketing activities across Ecosire, Odovation, and MuhammadAmir.com.

---

## Overview

Ecosire operates an AI Content Engine that produces SEO-optimized content for its web properties. The strategy encompasses traditional SEO, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), and LLM Optimization.
