# Odoo Community vs Enterprise

> Detailed comparison guide — originally published on [[MuhammadAmir.com]].

---

## Overview

Odoo offers two editions: Community (free, open-source) and Enterprise (paid, full-featured). The choice depends on business size, budget, and feature requirements.

---

## Key Differences

| Feature | Community | Enterprise |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| **Price** | Free | Paid (per user/month) |
| **Source Code** | Open source (LGPL) | Proprietary additions |
| **Hosting** | Self-hosted | Odoo.sh, cloud, or self-hosted |
| **Studio** | Not available | Visual app builder |
| **Mobile** | Limited | Full mobile apps |
| **IoT** | Not available | IoT box integration |
| **Support** | Community forums | Official Odoo support |
| **Accounting** | Basic | Advanced (bank sync, budgets, assets) |
| **MRP** | Basic | Full (work centers, quality, PLM) |
| **Website Builder** | Basic | Advanced themes and eCommerce |

---

## When to Choose Community
- Small businesses with basic needs
- Technical teams who can self-support
- Budget-conscious startups
- Simple CRM, invoicing, and inventory

## When to Choose Enterprise
- Growing businesses needing advanced features
- Companies requiring official support
- Complex manufacturing or multi-company setups
- Advanced accounting and financial reporting needs
