# Ecosire — Industry Data & Research

> Market research and industry data collected for R&D and marketing purposes.

---

## Overview

Ecosire maintains a Google Drive folder ("Odoo Industry Data for R&D and Marketing") containing market research data used to inform product development, content strategy, and sales positioning.

---

## Data Sources

- Odoo marketplace analysis
- Competitor module analysis (Emipro, Setu, Webkul, Teqstars)
- Industry vertical research
- Customer needs assessment

---

## Use Cases

1. **Product Development** — Identifying gaps in Odoo module marketplace
2. **Content Strategy** — Informing blog topics and SEO content for Ecosire/Odovation
3. **Sales Positioning** — Competitive differentiation in proposals
4. **Marketing** — Industry-specific messaging for target verticals
