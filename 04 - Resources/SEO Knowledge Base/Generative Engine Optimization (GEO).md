# Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

> Optimizing content to be selected and cited in AI-generated responses (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Bing Copilot).

---

## What is GEO?

GEO optimizes content for inclusion in AI-generated responses. When an AI system generates an answer, it selects and synthesizes information from multiple sources. GEO ensures your content is selected and cited.

---

## Five Principles of GEO

1. **SEO fundamentals first** — Pages must rank in traditional search before AI considers them
2. **Entity clarity** — Precise entity names, defined terms, authoritative references
3. **Content extractability** — Discrete facts, statistics, and claims extractable without full-page context
4. **Cross-platform presence** — Consistent information across website, social media, directories
5. **AI metrics tracking** — Citation rate, Share of Model, AI Overview inclusion

---

## Content Methods & Impact

| Method | Visibility Boost | Implementation |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| Statistics & data | +30–40% | Min 2 stats per section, cite sources |
| Citations & references | +40% | Min 1 citation per factual claim |
| Authoritative language | +15–20% | Confident tone, definitive statements |
| Fluency & readability | +10–15% | Clear prose, logical flow |
| Technical terminology | +5–10% | Precise industry terms + plain-language explanations |
| Keyword stuffing | NEGATIVE | Degrades AI citation probability |

---

## GEO Content Rules

- At least 2 statistics per major section with source citations
- At least 1 citation per factual claim — link to primary source
- Self-contained paragraphs (each expressible as standalone cite)
- Entity-first sentences ("React Server Components allow..." not "When building...")
- No filler phrases or hedging — confident, direct, evidence-backed

---

## Share of Model Metric

Measures how often your brand/content is cited in AI-generated responses vs. competitors. Track by:
- Querying AI systems with target keywords
- Comparing citation counts against competitor domains
- Monitoring changes over time
