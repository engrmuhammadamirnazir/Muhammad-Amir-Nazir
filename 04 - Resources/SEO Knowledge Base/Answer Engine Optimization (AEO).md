# Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

> Optimizing for featured snippets, People Also Ask, voice search, and AI-generated answers.

---

## What is AEO?

AEO focuses on winning positions where search engines extract and display direct answers from your content — featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results. It builds on SEO but emphasizes question-answering, structure, and authority in synthesized answers.

---

## Featured Snippet Targeting

- Provide a direct answer of 40–60 words above the fold
- Structure: Question as H2/H3 heading + concise paragraph answer
- Answer must be self-contained (works when extracted alone)
- Include target question verbatim in heading

### Snippet Format Types

| Format | Structure | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Definition | 40–60 word paragraph | Concepts, overviews |
| List | 3–8 items ordered/unordered | Steps, tips, reasons |
| Table | HTML table with header + 3+ rows | Comparisons, data |
| Steps | Numbered list + HowTo schema | Tutorials, how-tos |

---

## Voice Search Optimization

- Conversational tone, natural when read aloud
- Target question phrases: who, what, where, when, why, how
- Spoken answers under 30 words
- Optimize for local queries ("near me", "[service] in [city]")
- Use Speakable schema for text-to-speech sections

---

## Key Tactics

1. Build a question inventory from audience research
2. Lead with 1–2 sentence answers, expand below
3. Use FAQ-style blocks with FAQPage schema
4. Implement HowTo schema for step-by-step content
5. Monitor People Also Ask data quarterly
